Our Story

SOLIDARITY BUILDING AND LANDSCAPE CONTRACTORS is a private company limited by shares registered and incorporated on the 27th day of February 2015 vide Certificate of Incorporation No. CPR/2015/179391 The company is also registered with the National Construction Authority (NCA) under the following Categories: (a) Building Works Contractor (b) Road Works Contractor – NCA 5 NCA 5 SOLIDARITY

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every project, no matter how big or small, deserves the highest level of craftsmanship and dedication. Today, with several successful completed projects and a team of dedicated professionals, we continue to set new standards in construction quality, landscaping innovation, and event excellence.

At Your Service With Lots of Love and Passion

SOLIDARITY BUILDING AND LANDSCAPE CONTRACTORS was established with the goal of taking the current flagship in construction and growing it within the region and beyond; by providing the trust and quality that our customers have learnt to rely on. Our main function is to provide building construction services to developers, organizations, government institutions, individuals as well as private clients. Our management and staff have a complimentary experience in building construction and landscaping services. SOLIDARITY BUILDING brings to the job multi disciplinary teams with requisite capacity and outstanding track record in undertaking & managing major construction projects. SOLIDARITY BUILDING envisages growth over time as it works towards its vision. Since its establishment, SOLIDARITY BUILDING has a reputation as an ideal partner for organizations seeking reliable contractors and this has made us see our clientele grow steadily.
